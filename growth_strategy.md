## 3. Marketing & Growth Strategies

### A. Local SEO (Google My Business) - **CRITICAL**
1.  **Create/Claim GMB Listing:** Verify your business on Google Maps.
2.  **Optimize Profile:** Add photos of your cars, office, and happy customers. Use keywords in the description.
3.  **Reviews:** Ask every customer to leave a 5-star review on Google. Reply to every review.
4.  **Posts:** Post weekly updates on GMB (e.g., "Weekend Getaway Offer: 10% off on Ayodhya Trips").

### B. Social Media (Instagram/Facebook)
- **Reels:** Create short videos of your clean cars, drivers in uniform, and scenic routes (e.g., "Road trip to Varanasi").
- **Offers:** "First Ride Flat ₹100 Off" or "Airport Drop Special Rate".
- **Testimonials:** Share screenshots of WhatsApp feedback from happy clients.

### C. Partnerships (B2B)
- **Hotels:** Partner with hotels in Lucknow/Varanasi. Give them a commission for every guest they book with you.
- **Travel Agents:** Network with local travel agents who need reliable cabs for their tour packages.
- **Event Planners:** Offer bulk booking rates for weddings and corporate events.

### D. WhatsApp Marketing
- Save every customer's number.
- Use WhatsApp Business Broadcast lists (do not spam).
- Send status updates about availability and offers (e.g., "Empty car returning from Gorakhpur to Lucknow - 50% off").

### E. Paid Ads (When Budget Allows)
- **Google Search Ads:** Target "Cab service [City]" keywords. High intent, immediate results.
- **Facebook/Insta Ads:** Target people interested in "Travel" and "Tourism" in UP.

## 4. Technical Improvements for Next.js
- **Speed:** Ensure images are optimized (WebP format).
- **Mobile Friendly:** 90% of bookings will come from mobile. Ensure buttons are easily clickable.
- **Analytics:** Install Google Analytics 4 and Search Console to track traffic and keywords.
